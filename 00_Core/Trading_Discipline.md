# Trading Discipline

Atlas trading discipline converts research into position behavior.

## Core Rules

- Do not trade headlines directly.
- Do not confuse signal with action.
- Do not increase position size without high confidence and high risk/reward.
- Do not chase if the thesis is right but the price has already moved too far.
- Use cash as optionality, not as failure.
- If facts change, update the model before defending the position.

## Minimum Trade Check

Every trade must answer:

1. What changed?
2. Which layer of the reasoning chain changed?
3. Which bottleneck or ROI path changed?
4. Why is the current price still attractive?
5. What is the counter argument?
6. What will invalidate the trade?
