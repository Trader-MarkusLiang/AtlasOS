# Timing Engine

The Timing Engine links framework conviction to entry discipline.

## Basic Timing Questions

1. Is the industry direction intact?
2. Has the bottleneck state improved, weakened, or stayed the same?
3. Is capital already crowded in the target?
4. Is the current move thesis-driven or emotion-driven?
5. Does the current price still offer favorable risk/reward?

## Default Timing Rules

| Condition | Action |
|---|---|
| Thesis improves and price pulls back | Prepare to add |
| Thesis improves and price accelerates vertically | Avoid chasing; wait for reset |
| Thesis weakens and price rises | Reduce confidence |
| Thesis unchanged and market noisy | Hold discipline |
| Facts change against thesis | Rebuild model before trading |
