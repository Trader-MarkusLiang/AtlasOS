# Capital Rotation Table

Capital Rotation Table tracks when capital should move between themes.

| From | To | Trigger | Evidence | Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Memory | Equipment | Memory crowded; equipment order certainty improves | Equipment ranked S+ and relay path confirms next stage | Prepare rotation watchlist; add only on reset or order evidence |
| Equipment | Materials | Equipment move matures; material constraints strengthen | Materials ranked S and benefit from process intensity | Watch qualification, pass-through, and margin evidence |
| Materials | Bandwidth | Optical / interconnect bottleneck becomes first-level | Bandwidth ranked S and Corning case upgrades bottleneck | Build beneficiary map; avoid chasing vertical moves |
| Cash | High-conviction bottleneck | Price reset + thesis intact | Risk Radar clear of high-severity blockers | Deploy in batches according to Capital Allocation Board |

## Rotation Rule

Rotation is allowed only when:

1. The destination bottleneck is confirmed in the current ranking snapshot.
2. The source position is crowded, extended, or lower risk/reward.
3. Evidence exists in Alpha Radar, Order Book, Price Transmission, or a case replay.
4. Risk Radar does not show an unresolved high-severity blocker.
