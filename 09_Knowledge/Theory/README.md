# Theory Layer

Theory is the highest layer of the Atlas Knowledge Pyramid.

```text
Theory
 ↑
Pattern
 ↑
Case
 ↑
Evidence
 ↑
Signal
```

## Definition

Theory is a long-term, emergent explanation of how markets work.

Theory is not a single insight, thesis, framework, or investment idea. It is what remains after
multiple Patterns have been validated across years, industries, and cycles.

## Formation Conditions

Theory can form only when all conditions are met:

- Multiple Patterns support the same higher-order logic.
- The Patterns have been validated over multiple years.
- The logic works across industries.
- The logic works across market cycles.
- The logic can help predict future market structure.
- Counter examples and invalid conditions are known.

## Creation Rule

Theory cannot be actively created.

Theory cannot be designed.

Theory must emerge.

Do not create Theory because Atlas wants a more elegant architecture. Theory can only appear after
long-term operation proves that multiple Patterns share a deeper law.
