# Knowledge Philosophy

Atlas does not accumulate information.

Atlas distills reusable reasoning patterns.

Atlas does not collect news.

Atlas continuously updates its understanding of the world.

Atlas 不积累信息。

Atlas 沉淀可复用的推理模式。

Atlas 不收集新闻。

Atlas 持续更新自己对世界的理解。

## Core Distinction

News is not Knowledge.

News is Signal or Evidence. Knowledge begins only when Atlas extracts reusable reasoning, pattern,
decision logic, case learning, or World Model change from evidence.

## Atlas Knowledge Pyramid

```text
Theory
 ↑
World Model
 ↑
Pattern
 ↑
Case
 ↑
Evidence
 ↑
Signal
```

| Layer | Lifecycle | Role | Default |
|---|---|---|---|
| Signal | Hours to days | Triggers research. News, announcements, X, Reddit, calls, meetings, channel checks, and price action live here first. | Archive unless upgraded |
| Evidence | Weeks to months | Validates or rejects Signal. Orders, ASP, backlog, CapEx, FCF, earnings, and official announcements live here. | Keep until decision or review resolves it |
| Case | Years | Validates Pattern. A Case is a reusable decision episode with background, evidence, reasoning, decision, outcome, and lessons. | Promote only when it tests a Pattern |
| Pattern | Long term | Reusable logic extracted from multiple Cases. Every Pattern must belong to a World Model Node. | Merge only after review |
| World Model | Active long term | Atlas's current understanding of the AI world. It organizes Patterns, Cases, Evidence, and Signals into domains and nodes. | Update only after evidence, reasoning, and review |
| Theory | Emergent, long term | A Theory is formed only after multiple Patterns remain stable across years, industries, and cycles. | No active creation |

## Layer Relationships

- Signal triggers research.
- Evidence validates Signal.
- Case validates Pattern.
- Pattern is extracted from multiple Cases.
- World Model organizes Patterns into Atlas's current understanding of the AI world.
- Theory emerges from multiple long-lived Patterns.

Theory cannot be designed.

Theory must emerge.

Theory does not participate in daily workflow. It can only appear after long-term Atlas operation.

## Case Upgrade Criteria

A Signal or Evidence chain can become a Case only if it satisfies at least one condition:

- First validates a new Pattern.
- Refutes an old Pattern.
- Establishes a new Capital Relay.
- Establishes a new Bottleneck.
- Changes trading discipline.
- Affects more than one year of decisions.

## Pattern Requirements

A Pattern must be reusable across:

- Companies.
- Industries.
- Cycles.

World Model is the highest active subject of Atlas Knowledge.

Pattern is a reusable component inside a World Model Node.

Examples include:

- Capacity Constraint.
- Pricing Power.
- Capital Relay.
- Infrastructure Before Application.
- Efficiency Multiplier.
- Supply Chain Verification.
- Platform Transition.
- Jevons Effect.
- HBM Supercycle.

## Company Rule

Companies are not the primary unit of Atlas knowledge.

Companies are instances of Patterns.

Examples:

- Micron can be an instance of HBM Supercycle, Pricing Power, and Memory Constraint.
- Corning can be an instance of Bandwidth, Materials, and Infrastructure.
- DeepSeek can be an instance of Efficiency Multiplier and Inference Expansion.

## Repository Rule

Repository commits should represent Knowledge Merge, not News Archive.

Knowledge Merge should ultimately explain how Atlas World Model changed or why it did not change.

## Theory Rule

Theory is not a planning artifact.

Theory is not a framework design task.

Theory can only emerge from:

- Multiple Patterns.
- Years of validation.
- Cross-industry usefulness.
- Cross-cycle survival.
- Ability to predict future market structure.

Current status: no Theory yet.
